About us

Paghdal Parivar is formed in the spirit of bringing communities together, raising resources for the long-term future, nurturing relationships and connections that build a sense of community. we started our journey in 2013. we allow showcasing the talent and bringing the world closer together.

The Covid-19 has shut down the normalcy of the world, bringing in the new normal- social distancing, virtual spaces and newer means of communication and keeping up. Covid altered our lives over a bend but its not the end. What if we cannot meet physically and pursue our passions, our home is full of possibilities- from growing own veggies to flowers, trying out new dishes, learning a new language and DIY crafts, name it and you can possibly do it in your way. The world is under lockdown, but not our talents. We together are here to showcase your passion and interests to the world. Tried a new dish or added a personal twist to the traditional one- send in the new recipe and spread the delicious taste, at times when relatives cannot visit your home. Or express your love for poetry with the live poetry sessions, for the audience is ready to listen to you. Mesmerize everyone with your sweet voice and musical instruments or stun with your dance moves. The platform is yours, go on with all you want to do, for the life is yours and you shape it in your style.

‘Why limit yourselves to the sky, when you can reach the stars, we are there for you and your interests.’
